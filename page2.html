    High definition maps are at the heart of many kinds of robotics innovation, e.g. spatial computing, autonomous vehicles, IoT to name a few. We believe this will happen again now in 3D, 4D, XD.
    Once the world is mapped in HD, a new generation of robots will flourish. With spacial definition of our environment, comes a whole new level of efficiency for humanity - a renaissance for mobility solutions. Technology has control, and it is time we take that back. For cities this means an opportunity to retrofit and re-engineer towards adaptation for the future.
    3DCityIO [XD Map™] technology represents the culmination of speed, and high definition 3D mapping accuracy with ancillary sensor overlay.
