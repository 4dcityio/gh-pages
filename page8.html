What we quantify, is what we can improve



3DCityIO is focusing on creating high definition 3D maps of the urban environment, with contextual data overlays - or what we are calling,
[XD Maps™]
These are custom, on-demand, Maps-as-a-Service™ products

First target market : Last Mile Logisitics

There are increasingly more delivery robot startup companies popping up all over the world. Already there is pushback from city planners, and inhabitants as there is a challenge to find space for these amazing innovations.  3DCityIO is set to define where robots and humans should share space - in what we refer to as creating virtual 'robot corridors', or logistics paths that play well with the humans.  This solution space + real city flow context data are essentially 3D maps with contextual overlays - refererred to as [XD Maps™].

Starting with mobility [e..g. last mile logisitics], we aim to provide robotics companies with a custom service and product, delivered in the form of [XD Maps™] data product. We hope that under certain agreements, public companies will allow for a publicly accessible resource called [City A.I™]. [City A.I™] will help visualize and input these map trajectories, and find that optimal path for delivery taking all accumilated data into account.  These efforts are simply focused on improving their delivery services, but with partnering closely with robot manufacturers and cities, we can help create that consortium necessary to help the entire industry move forward in a responible and expeditious way., help them expand their particular reach, and improve the quality of their trajectory - thereby helping to optimize their service corridors.
